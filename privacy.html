Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, upload invoices, or contact customer support. This includes standard invoice data (vendor names, line items, pricing) required to perform our service.

2. How We Use Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the LineItem Logic service. We do not sell your data to third parties. Your invoice data is processed programmatically to extract information and sync it to your QuickBooks Online account.

3. QuickBooks Integration

Our service integrates with QuickBooks Online ("Intuit"). By using our service, you grant us access to read and write data to your QuickBooks account specifically for the purpose of creating bills and managing vendors.

4. Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration and destruction.
